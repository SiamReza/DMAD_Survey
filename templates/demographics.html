Master's Thesis Research Survey

Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU)

You are invited to participate in a research study for a Master's thesis in the Department of Computer Science (IDI) at Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) . This survey is designed to conduct a human evaluation on enhanced facial morph images.

Your task will be to view 100 pairs of images and, on a scale of 1-5, rate how sure you are that they are pictures of the same person.

The survey is expected to take approximately 15 minutes. Your participation is completely voluntary and anonymous. All data collected will be used for research purposes only, and no personal information will be stored.

Thank you for your valuable contribution to this academic research.
